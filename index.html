Saint Michael College Of Caraga/Services

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC) offers a variety of services to support the academic and personal development of its students:

Student Affairs and Services:
The Office of Student Affairs and Services (OSAS) is dedicated to promoting awareness and advocacy on important matters related to student life. They organize programs and activities designed to assist students in achieving their academic and personal goals.

Learning Resource Center:
SMCC's Learning Resource Center (LRC) provides access to a wide range of educational materials to support students' research and learning needs. The LRC aims to be a comprehensive library and information environment that underpins the institution's educational, research, and extension endeavors.

Student Portal:
The college offers an online Student Portal where students can access academic information, course materials, and other resources essential for their studies. Guidance on how to access and utilize the portal is available to ensure students can effectively navigate the platform.
